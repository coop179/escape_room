You've found the documents! Now you need to plan your escape. You consult with Neptune and he agrees to give you safe passage back to Pittsburg. However, you need to find a ship, and not just any ship, but something that would be able to hide you from the Greek gods who stole the documents. As you search the shores of the Aegean Sea, you find a rather large opening. You venture into the dark space to find a submarine, but it is being guarded by Agamemnon. You must find a way to distract him, open the door to the sub, and escape. You are so close!
